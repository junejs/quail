# AI Music Generation Prompts for Quail Quiz

Use these prompts in AI music generation tools like Suno, Udio, or Stable Audio to create custom assets for your project.

## 1. Lobby (Waiting Room)
*   **Style**: Upbeat, Synth-pop, Anticipation, Catchy, Electronic.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "An upbeat and catchy synth-pop background track for a game lobby. High energy, rhythmic electronic drums, bright synthesizers, building anticipation and excitement. Loopable, no vocals, 120 BPM."

## 2. Question (During Gameplay)
*   **Style**: Tense, Percussive, Minimalist, Clock-ticking, Driving.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "A tense and rhythmic percussive track for a quiz game countdown. Minimalist electronic pulses, a subtle clock-ticking sound in the background, driving bassline that increases in intensity. Suspenseful, no melody, focused on rhythm."

## 3. Correct (Success SFX)
*   **Style**: Bright, Reward, Sparkle, Success, Short.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "A short, bright, and rewarding sound effect for a correct answer. Shimmering chimes, a triumphant rising synth chord, positive and celebratory. High-pitched, 2 seconds long, crystal clear."

## 4. Incorrect (Fail SFX)
*   **Style**: Playful, Fail, Dull, Descending, Cartoonish.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "A short, playful 'fail' sound effect for an incorrect answer. A descending slide whistle or a dull, low-pitched synth 'bloop'. Slightly disappointing but funny, 1.5 seconds long."

## 5. Podium (Victory/Results)
*   **Style**: Triumphant, Grand, Orchestral, Victory, High-energy.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "A grand and triumphant victory theme for a game podium. Orchestral brass mixed with high-energy electronic beats, celebratory fanfares, epic and inspiring. Feeling of achievement, no vocals, 130 BPM."

## 6. Join (Player Joined SFX)
*   **Style**: Pop, Satisfying, Welcome, Digital, Quick.
*   **Prompt**: 
    > "A quick and satisfying digital 'pop' or 'ping' sound effect for a player joining a room. Welcoming, friendly, modern UI sound, high-fidelity, 0.5 seconds long."

---

**Tips for Generation:**
*   **General Style**: If the tool has a separate "Style" field, use: `Game Audio, High Fidelity, Electronic, 48kHz`.
*   **Looping**: For Lobby, Question, and Podium, ensure you specify "Loopable" or "Seamless Loop" if the tool supports it.
